The top 6

The names with a story.

Pick One
lawhand.ai
The hand behind the work.

"Law-hand" is a real historical term — the ornate handwriting that scribes used to draft legal documents centuries before typewriters existed. The name carries its own story: it's the hand that does the writing, while the attorney does the thinking, the meeting, and the courtroom. Built-in metaphor, no explaining needed.

Feels like: a master scribe who already knows your house style and has the brief ready before you ask.
Pick Two
firmhand.ai
Steady. Decisive. Always there.

Two meanings, one wordmark. A firm hand is the kind of help that doesn't second-guess itself — and a law firm runs on having someone reliable in every chair. Reads professional without trying hard. The cleanest trademark surface of the six: nothing else in legal-AI has claimed this metaphor.

Feels like: the senior paralegal who never lets a deadline slip and never makes the same mistake twice.
Pick Three
briefclerk.ai
Briefs, motions, exhibits — drafted overnight.

Says exactly what it does. "Brief" is the unit of lawyer work; "clerk" is the role that prepares everything for the attorney to sign. Of the six names here, this is the one a stranger understands in one breath. No explanation, no metaphor, no story to unpack. The product is the name.

Feels like: a clerk who never sleeps, never asks for a raise, and never types the wrong case number.
Pick Four
lawowl.ai
Wisdom that doesn't blink.

The owl is the oldest symbol of wisdom in Western culture — Athena's owl, the watchful judge, the bird that sees in the dark. This is the one name with a built-in visual identity opportunity: every law firm has bookshelves and brass statues, but no one's claimed the owl as a software mark. Sticky, ownable, distinct.

Feels like: the wisdom that was waiting for you when you logged in at 11pm with a motion due at nine.
Pick Five
casehand.ai
A hand for every case.

Turns one of the strongest cues in this set — the "hand" metaphor — into a per-case promise. Every matter gets its own dedicated AI hand: knows the timeline, the parties, the next deadline. Naturally builds a brand family with lawhand and firmhand — three "hands" that scale with how lawyers already think about their work.

Feels like: a paralegal assigned to every case the day it opens, who never forgets a name, a date, or a footnote.
Pick Six
legalowl.ai
The owl that never sleeps.

The boardroom-ready sibling of lawowl. Same Athena's-owl metaphor — classical legal wisdom, distinct visual identity — but "legal" reads more formal than "law" the way "attorney" reads more formal than "lawyer." Fits enterprise procurement, GC offices, and partner-level conversations. Strongest case for it: register both so a competitor can't fork the metaphor against you later.

Feels like: the same owl — in a pressed shirt and cufflinks.
