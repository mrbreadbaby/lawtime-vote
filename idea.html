The idea

A clerk who does everything except show up to court.

Every name we're voting on is for the same thing: an AI that drafts your briefs, pulls your exhibits, indexes your discovery, watches your calendar, and never asks for a raise.

Think of it as the senior paralegal who never sleeps — the one who's already pulled the chronology before you ask, knows every party in every matter, and has the next motion drafted by morning.

01
Drafts your briefs
Motions, declarations, demand letters — in your firm's voice, with proper citations.
02
Indexes discovery
Reads everything, tags it, tells you what matters and what's missing.
03
Watches the calendar
Tracks deadlines, hearings, statutes of limitations. Nothing slips.
04
Pulls the exhibits
Finds the doc, marks the page, builds the binder — ready to file.

The attorney does the judgment.
The clerk does the rest.

Court appearances, client conversations, the strategic calls — those stay with you. Everything else — the prep, the pulling, the drafting, the tracking — gets handled.
